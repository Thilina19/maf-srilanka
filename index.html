Our Story

Four Decades of Athletic Excellence

The Mercantile Athletic Federation (M.A.F) was founded on 7th September 1982 at the Ceylon Tours office on Parsons Road, Colombo 02, with the support of over 30 mercantile sector firms. What began as a modest initiative has grown into Sri Lanka's most prestigious corporate athletics body.

The inaugural championship was held on November 6-7, 1982 at Shalika Grounds, attracting over 600 athletes from 50+ firms. Since then, the MAF has been the heartbeat of mercantile athletics — promoting track & field, road races, masters athletics, and netball across the island.

MAF was the first organization to introduce veterans (masters) athletics in Sri Lanka in 1985, and has represented the nation at World Veterans Games, Asian Masters Championships, and numerous international competitions.
